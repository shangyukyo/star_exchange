Stage en Chine - Déroulement de la candidature

 

The application process and the preparations for your stay in China comprise several steps:

  1. Inform yourself on our homepage
  2. Ask potential questions, here
  3. Complete our application form, here
  4. We will review your application and forward it to your account manager in China
  5. You and your account manager will talk about your motivation and personal goals
  6. Matching and interview
  7. Register for the Intern in China Program
  8. Preparations for your stay in China
  9. Arrival in China
