Teacher in China

Application

To sign up for the Mandarin School Teacher in China program, please use our online application below.

Upon receipt of your application you will receive a confirmation email. Usually you will be notified after one week if your application has been successful or not.

Alternatively you can apply via our manual application sheet. Simply download the PDF "Manual Application" here and send it together with the other documents (as mentioned in the PDF) to application-teacher@starexchange.eu

However, the processing of the manual application may require three times as long as the online application.
